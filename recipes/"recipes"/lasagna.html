Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and various seasonings. It is baked to perfection, resulting in a flavorful and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for a few minutes.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in a baking dish. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles on top.
  8. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and cheese on top.
  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  11. Allow the lasagna to cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
