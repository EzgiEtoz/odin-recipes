Potato Soup

Warm and comforting, potato soup is a classic dish that's perfect for chilly days.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the diced potatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer until the potatoes are tender.
  3. Using a blender or immersion blender, puree the soup until smooth and creamy.
  4. Return the soup to the pot, stir in the milk, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes, then serve hot.
